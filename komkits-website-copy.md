**Section label:** AI-NATIVE PLATFORM

**Headline:**
## Your Business Data, Speaking Your Language

**Intro:**
KomKits isn't a business tool with AI bolted on. It's built AI-first — so your data works for you, wherever you work.

**Feature blocks:**

### 🔑 Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)
Plug in your own AI API key and build custom reports right inside KomKits. Ask questions in plain language — get answers from your real business data. No data lock-in, no per-seat AI pricing. Your key, your control.

### 🔌 MCP: Connect KomKits to Your AI Tools
KomKits ships with a built-in MCP server — meaning ChatGPT, Claude, or any MCP-compatible AI assistant can securely connect to your business data. Ask your AI assistant *"What were my top-selling items last month?"* and get real answers, straight from KomKits.

### 📊 AI-Powered Reports
Skip the spreadsheet gymnastics. Describe the report you need, and KomKits builds it — sales trends, expense breakdowns, payment summaries, and more.
