What ships: the carousel replaces the desktop step column under @media (max-width: …md); the pinned scrub markup stays untouched above it (Twenty's layoutMode: 'scroll' | 'swipe' fork). Snap position → active index is one scroll listener (round(scrollLeft / cardWidth)); no drag JS — the browser owns the physics.
