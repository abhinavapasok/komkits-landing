FAQ

Questions, answered

Is the free plan really free?
Yes — the Seed plan is free forever with core billing, item management, and one user. You only pay when you need unlimited transactions, AI features, or more users.
Is my business data shared with other companies?
Never. Every business gets its own isolated database — your data is not pooled, shared, or used to train anything. Your own AI key means even AI processing stays under your control.
What does “Bring Your Own Key” mean?
You plug in your own AI API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) and KomKits uses it to build reports and answer questions on your data. No data lock-in, no per-seat AI pricing — your key, your control.
How does the ChatGPT / Claude connection work?
KomKits ships with a built-in MCP server. Connect it once, and any MCP-compatible assistant can securely query your business — ask “what were my top-selling items last month?” and get real answers.
When do the Shopify and WhatsApp integrations arrive?
They’re on the active roadmap — join the waitlist and we’ll email you the moment early access opens. We badge everything honestly: if it says “coming soon”, it’s not live yet.
How long does setup take?
Minutes. Sign up, verify your email, choose a business code, and your workspace is provisioned automatically — no sales call, no implementation project.
